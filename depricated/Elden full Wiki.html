Starter Gear

What is Starter Gear?

Starter Gear is a special set of 3 weapons and 1 armor set designed to help you level up quickly.

It's equivalent in stats to level 50 Daevanion gear, making it strong for early and mid-game content.

  • The gear cannot be enchanted or socketed, but:
    • You can enchant and socket the weapons
    • You can armfuse them with another Starter or e50 weapon for extra power

Note: This gear is completely optional. Feel free to skip it if you prefer a more classic experience!

Where to get it?

You'll receive the full set by completing the "A Ceremony in Pandaemonium" (Asmo) or "A Ceremony in Sanctum" (Elyos) quest – right after Ascension!

Lumen

What are Lumen?

Lumen are Elden Aion Shop Currency used to buy cosmetics and useful Items from the Lumen Shop

Ways to obtain Lumen:

  1. Go to website and login
  2. Click on "Vote Rewards"
  3. Select & Follow the steps inside every voting website

Instances between level 50-55 drop fewer Lumen, while those between levels 56-60 drop the most, depending on their difficulty

Events like Shugo Tomb & Circus may drop Lumen from chests

You can obtain Lumen from surveys during leveling or events

You can receive Lumen by Streaming on Twitch or Youtube

How do I use Lumen?

  1. Log out from Elden Aion (Character Selection works too),
  2. Login on website
  3. Move to Shop
  4. Select your character name
  5. Select the amount of Lumens you want to Exchange and click on "Add Lumen"

Gift Codes

What are Gift Codes?

Gift Codes are special promo codes shared weekly by the Administrator in the @gift-codes channel (also available on the official website).

They can be redeemed for various rewards like mounts, cosmetics, skins, and more!

Note: Gift codes are seasonal and expire after a while, so don't miss them!

How to redeem Gift Codes?

  1. Go to: https://eldenaion.com/home
  2. Log in with your account credentials
  3. Click your username in the top-right corner
  4. Select "Gift Code" from the dropdown
  5. Enter the code (make sure it includes the #)
  6. Now go to: https://eldenaion.com/shop
  7. Choose your character name
  8. Click "Claim Gift"

Your rewards will be delivered via Express Mail in-game!

Atreia Pass

What is Atreia Pass?

The Atreia Pass is a non-pay-to-win (non-PZW) loyalty system. By completing missions and running instances, players earn tokens that allow them to progress through the pass. These tokens can be exchanged on the official website for exclusive rewards.

Rewards include cosmetics, consumables, and character-enhancing items. The system is designed to keep players engaged by offering valuable bonuses in return for their time and dedication.

Ways to obtain Atreia Pass Tokens

  1. Staying online
    For every hour you spend in-game, you earn 1 token
  2. Completing Campaign Quests
    Finish yellow campaign quests to receive additional tokens (one time only)
  3. Defeating Final Bosses
    Earn tokens by killing final bosses in endgame instances and open-world content
  4. Doing Daily & Weekly Quests
    Tokens are also rewarded for completing daily and weekly quests, as well as certain normal (blue) quests.

→ You can find the full list of quests that give tokens here

Exchanging Atreia Pass Tokens

  1. Log out from the game (Character Selection works too)
  2. Go to Elden Aion Website and log in
  3. Click on "Atreia Pass"
  4. Select your character name
  5. Click on "Exchange Tokens"
  6. Based on the amount exchanged, click the bonuses — they will be delivered via Express Mail in-game

Prism Core

What are Prism Cores?

  • Prism Core (32)
    • Untradeable
    • Unsellable
    • Unstorable in Account Warehouse
    • Unstorable in Legion Warehouse
    • Indestructible

An exchange currency that can be used to acquire Fleet Commander's Ceremonial equipment from the Novice Equipment Mentor found in every major city.

Prism Core is a special currency earned primarily by completing campaign (yellow) quests. The number of Cores you receive increases as you progress through higher-level campaign content.

These Cores can be exchanged for Fleet Commander's Equipment Boxes—a full Level 50 PvP gear set (E50)—through specific NPCs.

Exchange Rates

Item Cost
Full Set (5 pieces) 100 Cores
Weapon (1 piece) 10 Cores
Total (1 weapon) 110 Cores
Total (2 weapons) 120 Cores

Notes

  • If you'd prefer not to skip too much content, you can simply focus on progressing through the Campaign and some blue quests
  • Don't forget: XP amulets are easy to obtain! Just vote for the server daily to earn Lumen, which you can use to get XP boosts. Additionally, you'll receive Free Siel's Blessings every 2 hours (up to 2 per day)
