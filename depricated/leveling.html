Notes

  • Be sure to complete some Campaign (yellow) quests to collect enough Prism Cores for the Level 50 AP gear (E50 set)
  • If you'd prefer not to skip too much content, you can simply focus on progressing through the Campaign and some blue quests
  • Don't forget: XP amulets are easy to obtain! Just vote for the server daily to earn Lumen, which you can use to get XP boosts. Additionally, you'll receive Free Siel's Blessings every 2 hours (up to 2 per day)
  • Pro tip: You can create a second account to hold a group for yourself if you want to solo instances
  • Save all Coins (Silver, Gold, Platinum) – they can be used to buy PvP accessories from Coin Chests available at camp NPCs

Side Activities

These are useful tasks you can complete while leveling to ensure a smooth and efficient start on the server

Platinum Coins

You can get e50 AP accessories from Platinum Coin Chests that will serve as your starter PvP accessories. You can find a full guide here.

Daily Reward

Don't forget to claim your daily reward from the Shop – it includes potions, scrolls, and a Level 85 Enchantment Stone. You will find the daily reward in "Atreia Reward" tab in in-game shop

Starter Manastones

As you level, look out for special pink quests. These provide bonus EXP and reward you with Level 50 Manastones, perfect for early endgame gearing. A full guide is available here.
