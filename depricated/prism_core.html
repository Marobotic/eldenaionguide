Prism Core

What are Prism Cores?

  • Prism Core (32)
    • Untradeable
    • Unsellable
    • Unstorable in Account Warehouse
    • Unstorable in Legion Warehouse
    • Indestructible

An exchange currency that can be used to acquire Fleet Commander's Ceremonial equipment from the Novice Equipment Mentor found in every major city.

Prism Core is a special currency earned primarily by completing campaign (yellow) quests. The number of Cores you receive increases as you progress through higher-level campaign content.

These Cores can be exchanged for Fleet Commander's Equipment Boxes—a full Level 50 PvP gear set (E50)—through specific NPCs.

Exchange Rates

Item Cost

Weapon (1 piece) 10 Cores
