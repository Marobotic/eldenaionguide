Introduction into Aion 3.9

Aion 3.9 – The Legendary Patch

Aion 3.9 is one of the most memorable patches in the history of the game, released during the golden era of MMORPGs. It brought new content, balance changes, and quality-of-life updates that shaped the way players experienced Atreia. For many veterans, 3.9 represents the peak of classic Aion, blending hardcore progression with a rich fantasy world, deep PvP systems, and engaging PvE challenges.

What Made Aion 3.9 Special?

1. Expanded Endgame Content

Patch 3.9 introduced new instances that pushed both solo and group players to their limits. These dungeons provided valuable rewards such as rare gear, crafting materials, and unique skins. Many players remember 3.9 as the patch that perfected dungeon variety—offering a balance between short, farmable runs and longer, raid-like challenges.

2. Housing & Customization

This update improved the housing system, allowing players to express themselves more fully. Owning and decorating a house wasn’t just cosmetic—it gave functional benefits like buffs and storage. The housing market also became a huge part of the in-game economy.

3. Mounts & Travel

With 3.9, Aion expanded its mount system, making travel across Atreia more stylish and efficient. Players could choose between different creatures, each with unique animations and appearances. This added another layer of personalization and status, since rare mounts became highly sought-after.

4. PvP Enhancements

Aion has always been known for its intense faction warfare between Elyos and Asmodians. In 3.9, PvP was more refined, with improved matchmaking for battlegrounds and better balance across classes. This kept the competitive community alive, as players strived to dominate arenas, dredgions, and large-scale sieges.

5. Class Balance & Meta

Patch 3.9 featured 8 main classes (Gladiator, Templar, Assassin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Spiritmaster, Chanter, and Cleric). Each class had its unique role in both PvP and PvE, and 3.9 was considered one of the more balanced periods of Aion’s history.

6. Quality-of-Life Changes

Alongside content, 3.9 smoothed out many rough edges of Aion. Features like faster teleportation, better UI updates, improved broker systems, and reduced grind in some areas made the game more enjoyable without losing its challenging nature.

Why Players Still Remember 3.9

For veterans, Aion 3.9 wasn’t just a patch—it was a golden memory. It was the perfect mix of hardcore MMO progression, rewarding grind, thrilling PvP, and community-driven experiences. The game world felt alive, factions were at constant war, and every dungeon run or PvP battle carried weight. Even today, many private servers and nostalgic discussions revolve around Aion 3.9. It remains a benchmark patch for what made MMORPGs so engaging—player-driven stories, high-stakes battles, and the thrill of earning every piece of gear through skill and dedication.

Getting Started with Elden Aion

Welcome to Elden Aion! This guide will walk you through creating an account, verifying it, downloading the game, and logging in for the first time.

For more details, visit the official website.

Use Only the Official Website

The only official Elden Aion website is eldenaion.com. Always start there to register and download the client. Using unofficial sites can put your account at risk.

1. Registration

  1. Go to eldenaion.com and click Register in the top-right corner.
  2. Fill out the registration form:
    • Username: your login name for the website and game
    • Email: a valid email you can access (required for verification)
    • Password: choose a secure password and keep it private
    • Confirm Password: re-enter your password to confirm

    Tip: Never share your password with anyone. You are fully responsible for your account security.

  3. After submitting, you’ll see a success message and receive a verification email.

2. Email Verification

  1. Open the verification email and click the link inside (or copy it into your browser).
  2. If the link doesn’t work, log in to the website and use the provided verification code:
  3. On the website, click the red banner prompt to open the PIN window. Enter your code and confirm.
  4. You’ll receive a confirmation message once your account is verified.

3. Downloading Elden Aion

  1. Go to the Download page and click the orange Download button. This guide covers the launcher installation method.
  2. Download the file eldenaion.exe and run it.
  3. If prompted to select an installation path, choose an SSD with at least 50 GB of free space. Recommended path: 
    C:\Games\Elden Aion

    If your boot drive is already an SSD with enough space, the default location is fine.

  4. Once installed, the launcher will look like this (appearance may vary):
  5. Enter your username and password in the launcher. The launcher will handle downloading the game files and future updates automatically.

4. First Launch & Choosing a Faction

When the download finishes, launch the game and choose your faction. Important: your faction choice is permanent and locked to your account. Elden Aion does not allow faction changes. If you’re unsure, join the community Discord and ask for advice.

Leveling guide for Elyos

Hello and welcome to the leveling guide for Elyos

Quick Leveling Path

For those experienced in this game, here's a quick reminder:

  • Lv1 to Lv10 Kill monsters in Poeta while moving to Pernos
  • Lv10 to Lv15 Kill monsters in Verteron
  • Lv15 to Lv21 Kill Verteron Kralis (at Ardus Shrine Entrance)
  • Lv21 to Lv25 Kill monsters in Novan's Crossing
  • Lv25 to Lv30 Nochsana Training Camp
  • Lv30 to Lv35 Little campaigns + Fire Temple Solo/duo (your choice)
  • Lv35 to Lv46 Go to Aetherogenic Lab + Campaign Quests
  • Lv46 to Lv53 Go to Adma Stronghold
  • Lv48 to Lv53 Alternative - Go to Dark Poeta (Clean all monsters) Solo (if Magical), Duo (if Physical)
  • Lv53 to Lv60 Go to Beshmundir Temple (Easy Mode) Solo (if Magical), Duo (if Physical)
  • Lv53 to Lv60 Alternative - Stay in Dark Poeta to farm money & complete Sarpan/Tiamaranta Campaign Quests

Detailed Leveling Guide

For those who need a more in-depth guide:

Starting Out

As soon as you load up with your newly created character, accept the surveys that appear next to your minimap.

Once you click on the pouch the window would appear, just press on Complete to receive your starting items.

It is recommended to use these following items, depending on your job:

  • Physical job: use physical scrolls
  • Magical class: use magical scrolls
  • Always use the running scroll

Also use your exp amulet and candy that is given to you. For the cube expansion, keep it and don't use it yet.

Title Selection

Make sure to use the title and equip it as it will boost your stats.

Level 1 to 10

Once you reach Akario village, make sure to talk to the NPC related to your class.

Then talk to Baevrunerk which is your first cube slot unlock.

If you only care about leveling up as fast as possible; here is the recommended path:

  • Ignore most quests and just kill mobs on your way to the ascension quest
  • Feel free to pick up yellow quests as you kill mobs and level up
  • Once you reach level 9, the ascension quest will be unlocked

You'll have to talk to Pernos and follow the quest line from that point:

  • Preta
  • Rafina
  • Hayman
  • Shoomia
  • Pernos PEP

At the end of ascension quest you're going to be prompted to select your class.

Once you choose your class, another ascension quest will appear, "A ceremony in Sanctum". Pick it up and follow the quest line until completion.

Once you complete the quest, you'll receive your starter gear and weapon.

Make sure to open these boxes, select your weapon and then equip your gear.

Elden Aion offers a custom starter gear appearance for your enjoyment!

You will keep this gear until you're max level 60. It is powerful and would allow you to solo most instances until max level.

Once you're done equipping your starter gear, the second stop for unlocking your cube will be in Sanctum with shugo Ylehmonerk or Heerunerk (either one works the same).

If you don't have enough Kinah for cube upgrades, you can ask a friend for spare kinah, or loot mobs you kill and sell items they drop until you have enough.

Level 10 to 16

Travel to Verteron using Sanctum porter.

Pick up any yellow quests on your way once you reach Verteron, as you will need these quests for later.

Note: The maximum level mobs you can kill is anything above you by 10 levels. So if you're level 10, maximum mob level you can kill is 20.

If you don't have enough kinah, kill mobs, loot them and sell the items quickly. At this point you'll be killing them fast, so you won't have much problems.

After that, fly to Haramel entrance. Mobs around there would be around 8-9 levels higher than you. Kill them until you reach level 16.

Level 16 to 21

Enter the Haramel instance and kill everything in there, following the dungeon path.

After clearing Haramel once, you should be around level 21-22.

Level 21 to 25

Go back to Verteron Citadel and then travel to Eltnen.

Once you make it to Eltnen, start moving to Novan's crossing, where you'll be killing open world mobs until you reach level 25.

Level 25+

Once you're level 25, you can enter your next instance: Nochsana Training Camp!

Find the instance entrance by talking to the NPC Rogun inside Eltnen Fortress.

You will need someone to hold group for you to access this instance, but you can clear it solo.

Options:

  1. Write in chat: /3 RecruitGroupMember hold group please!
  2. Dual client and hold group for yourself with another account

You can do this instance multiple times until you reach the EXP you want. Either do this until level 30, then do fire temple, or keep spamming this until level 40 - whichever you prefer.

Asmodian Level Guide

  • Lv1 to Lv9 Kill random monsters while moving to Munin (Class Change Quest)
  • Lv10 to Lv25 Hit mobs in Altgard until Lv16, then go to Haramel
  • Lv25 to Lv30 Nochsana Training Camp
  • Lv30 to Lv35–40 Do small campaigns + Fire Temple (solo/duo) or Alquimia Research Lab
  • Lv35–40 to Lv46 Grind campaigns (easy ones) — ensure enough Tribunus tokens; do Brusthonin quests after 45+
  • Lv46 to Lv48–50 Go to Theobomos Lab (clean main + left rooms), exit and repeat
  • Lv48–50 to Lv53 Go to Dark Poeta (clear all mobs) — solo (if magical), duo (if physical)
  • Lv53 to Lv60 Go to Beshmundir Temple (easy mode) — solo (if magical), duo (if physical)
  • Lv53 to Lv60 Alternative — stay in Dark Poeta to farm kinah and finish Sarpan/Tiamaranta campaigns

Starter Gear

What is Starter Gear?

Starter Gear is a special set of 3 weapons and 1 armor set designed to help you level up quickly. It’s equivalent in stats to level 50 Daevanion gear, making it strong for early and mid-game content.

NOTE: The gear can't be socketted or enchanted, but you can socket and enchant weapon

You can armfuse starter weapon with another starter wep or Level 50 abyss weapon (e50)

Note: This gear is completely optional. Feel free to skip it if you prefer a more classic experience!

Where to get it?

You'll receive the full set by completing the “A Ceremony in Pandaemonium” (Asmo) or “A Ceremony in Sanctum” (Elyos) quest — right after Ascension!

What are Lumen?

Lumen are Elden Aion Shop Currency used to buy cosmetics and useful Items from the Item Shop please visit https://eldenaion.com/shop after logging in to our website and have made a character. You can also access the shop from in-game shop icon next to the minimap

How to obtain Lumen?

Voting System

  • Go to the website and log in.
  • Click on Vote Rewards.
  • Select & follow the steps inside every voting website.

Instances

  • Instances between level 50–55 drop fewer Lumen.
  • Instances between level 56–60 drop the most, depending on their difficulty.

Events

  • Events like Shugo Tomb & Circus may drop Lumen from chests.

Surveys

  • You can obtain Lumen from surveys during leveling or events.

Streaming

  • You can receive Lumen by streaming on Twitch or YouTube.

What is Atreia Pass?

  • The Atreia Pass is a non-pay-to-win (non-P2W) loyalty system. By completing missions and running instances, players earn tokens that allow them to progress through the pass.
  • These tokens can be exchanged on the official website for exclusive rewards.
  • Rewards include cosmetics, consumables, and character-enhancing items.
  • The system is designed to keep players engaged by offering valuable bonuses in return for their time and dedication.

Ways to obtain Atreia Pass Tokens

  • Staying online — For every hour you spend in-game, you earn 1 token.
  • Completing Campaign Quests — Finish yellow campaign quests to receive additional tokens (one time only).
  • Defeating Final Bosses — Earn tokens by killing final bosses in endgame instances and open-world content.
  • Doing Daily & Weekly Quests — Tokens are also rewarded for completing daily and weekly quests, as well as certain normal (blue) quests.
  • → You can find the full list of quests that give tokens here.

Exchanging Atreia Pass Tokens

  • Log out from the game (Character Selection works too).
  • Go to the Elden Aion Website and log in.
  • Click on "Atreia Pass".
  • Select your character name.
  • Click on "Exchange Tokens".
  • Based on the amount exchanged, click the bonuses — they will be delivered via Express Mail in-game.

What are Prism Cores?

  • Prism Core is a special currency earned primarily by completing campaign (yellow) quests.
  • The number of Cores you receive increases as you progress through higher-level campaign content.
  • These Cores can be exchanged for Fleet Commander's Equipment Boxes — a full Level 50 PvP gear set (E50) — through specific NPCs.

About Spiritmaster:

What can I expect from playing a Spiritmaster in Ethereal Aion? Quick reflexes at the start of the battle, multitasking, and positioning strategy is essential to mastering the Spiritmaster class. If you are caught with your robes down, failure to quickly cast a shield or a Spirit Substitution spell might result in a quick defeat.

You will need to keep in mind that anything getting through the defense you will build up with debuffs and summons will cause you a lot of damage. Multitasking between you and your summon will be essential to the effective use of the class, requiring you to pay attention to the proper positioning as well as proper timing for actions involving both of the units.

Unique abilities of Spiritmaster:

Spirit Erosion skill increases the drop rate of rare items.

Unique ability to remove some of the buffs from bosses in higher-level instance areas.

Beyond all of that, Spiritmasters are still great secondary DPS dealers and are useful debuffers.
